Introduction

San Luis Coast Surf

San Luis Coast Surf is a small, local surf store in San Luis Obispo that has clothes, surfboards and other surf equipment for sale. It's based on sustainability and selling local brands and boards.

Target audience

The target audience of the website would be people in or visiting San Luis Obispo that are looking for a surf shop to buy clothes, boards and other surf equipment.

The target audience would visit the site to find the stores hours, find all contact information, the store location and the services provided.

About

We at San Luis Coast Surf Shop are fueled by creative freedom and a shared love for the ocean. Nestled in one of the most well-known surf communities in California, our crew has developed a unique modern approach inspired from classic, old school styles. In and out of the water, we promote self-expression and  are growing to influence others around the globe. In October of 2017 we opened our shop in San Luis Obispo, California. Our dream is for the shop to be a place where people can come hang out, talk surf, say hi and connect with what San Luis Coast Surf Shop is about.
